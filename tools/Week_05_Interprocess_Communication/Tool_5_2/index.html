📚 SOCK_STREAM vs SOCK_DGRAM
SOCK_STREAM — reliable, ordered, connection-oriented (like TCP). Data arrives in order, no message boundaries (byte stream).
SOCK_DGRAM — unreliable, unordered, connectionless (like UDP). Each send/recv is one discrete datagram; may be lost or reordered.
